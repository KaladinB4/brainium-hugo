About

Why Brainium exists and what we stand for.

Brainium is a research translation project. We read the papers, run the experiments, and extract what actually matters for people learning and working with AI.

What We Do

Research Synthesis: Breaking down studies on AI’s impact on cognition, learning, and skill development into actionable insights.

Framework Development: Building practical mental models for using AI productively—without cognitive offloading.

Field Testing: Documenting real experiments in learning with AI, with honest results.

Our Stance

We’re not anti-AI. We’re pro-intelligence.

The goal isn’t to avoid AI tools. It’s to use them in ways that amplify rather than replace your thinking.
